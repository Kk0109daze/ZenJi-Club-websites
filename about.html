About Us

About Our Story

ZenJi began with a simple idea: that everyone deserves a place of refuge. What started as small Monday night meditation circles among friends has blossomed into a vibrant, multi-location community.

Our mission is to be your haven—a dedicated space where you can find clarity, peace, and renewal. We’re proud to see adaptation of mindfulness techniques and breath practices. We’re more than a meditation club; we’re a partner in cultivating a more present and insightful life.

History

The idea of starting ZenJi flourished in the early 2010s, inspired by a shared passion for meditation and community. Our growth has been organic, rooted in the values of openness and support for all who seek stillness.

Our Growth on Campus

The evolution from 2015-2025 saw ZenJi embedded in campus culture, starting from one small room to multiple weekly gatherings and workshops. We’re grateful for the support of Sheridan College and the wider Oakville community.
